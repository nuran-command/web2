Tribute to Cristiano Ronaldo

About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo (born 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Known for his incredible goal-scoring record, athleticism, and determination, Ronaldo has achieved success at Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al Nassr, as well as the Portugal national team.

Life Events

  1. 1985 – Born in Madeira, Portugal.
  2. 2003 – Signed with Manchester United.
  3. 2009 – Transferred to Real Madrid, where he became the club's all-time top scorer.
  4. 2016 – Won UEFA Euro 2016 with Portugal.
  5. 2021 – Returned to Manchester United.

Key Achievements

  • 5× Ballon d'Or winner
  • UEFA Champions League all-time top scorer
  • Over 900 career goals
  • Multiple league titles across England, Spain, and Italy
